Pudding

Description

The brazilian pudding, known nationally as 'Pudim', is one of the most delicious and creamy deserts out there, with many flavour variants. This, however, is a recipe for the common "condesed milk pudding", very quick and easy to make, you are guaranteed to enjoy this delicious treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 can of sweet condensed milk
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of water

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs well
  2. Add the milk and sweet condensed milk into the mixture and keep beating using a blender.
  3. In a separate pan, melt the sugar until it gains a brown color.
  4. Add water and let it thicken.
  5. Pour the hot sugar into the round pan you will use for baking (make sure it coats the bottom of the pan well).
  6. Add the batter mixture on top of the caramelized sugar.
  7. Using a bigger pan filled with water under it, place your pan into the oven and let it bake for about 40-45 minutes.
  8. To make sure your pudding is properly baked, use a fork to stick the batter.
  9. After removing it from the oven, let it rest for about a hour.
  10. Use a knife to trim the edges of the pan, place a plate on top of it and flip it to unmold it.
  11. Call your family or friends to enjoy this delicious treat with you!
