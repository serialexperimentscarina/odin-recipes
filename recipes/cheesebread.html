Cheese Bread

Description

Some would say that eating a warm cheese bread over a cup of hot black coffee in a cold sunday morning is akin to going to heaven, so you might as well learn how to make this humble but delicious cheesy goodness.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/3 cup of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of tapioca flour
  • 1 and 1/2 cup of grated cheese
  • 3 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 C.
  2. In a pan on medium heat, mix the milk, water and oil, and bring it to a boil.
  3. On a separate bowl, mix the flour and salt.
  4. Combine both mixtures, stirring with a big spoon until all the liquid is incorporated.
  5. Let it cool.
  6. Add the cheese and eggs.
  7. Mix the batter using your hands (greased with butter or olive oil).
  8. Model the batter into tiny balls, placing them into your baking tray.
  9. Let it bake for about 40 minutes.
  10. Take them out of the oven and enjoy a piece of heaven in the form of a tiny cheese ball.
